Pick a Beat
Your choice of beat must reflect your mood
  1. Are you good with words?
  2. Can you Rhyme Multi-syllabically?
  3. Do you understand how prosody works?
  4. Can you go on a beat anchronistically?
  5. Do you have a story to tell?
